# Neosis - Smart Study Planner: Official User Guide

Welcome to **Neosis**, your futuristic, AI-powered study companion. This guide will walk you through everything you need to know to maximize your productivity, track your cognitive mastery, and achieve your academic goals.

---

## 🚀 1. Getting Started

### Authentication
When you first open Neosis, you will be prompted to log in. The app securely saves all your study data, schedules, and progress to the cloud so you can access it from anywhere without losing your hard work.

### The Dashboard Overview
Once logged in, you will be greeted by the Neosis Dashboard. Here you will find:
- **Subjects Panel:** Your active subjects and goal progress.
- **Schedule List:** Your tasks and study blocks for the day.
- **Pomodoro Timer:** The tool to execute your study sessions.
- **Analytics Chart:** Visual representation of your hard work.
- **AI Co-pilot:** Your intelligent assistant at the bottom of the screen.

---

## 📚 2. Setting Up Your Subjects

Before you start studying, you need to define what you are studying.

1. **Navigate to the Subjects Panel:** Look for the section displaying your courses.
2. **Add a Subject:** Click the "Add Subject" (or similar + icon) button.
3. **Define Your Goals:** 
   - Enter the **Subject Name** (e.g., Physics, Calculus).
   - Set your **Target/Goal Hours** (e.g., 40 hours for the semester).
   - *Optional:* You can mark a subject as a "Weak" subject if you need the AI to prioritize it later.
4. **Track Progress:** As you log study time, the progress bar for each subject will automatically fill up toward your goal.

---

## 📅 3. Creating Your Study Schedule

Planning is the key to consistency. You can manually plan your day or let the AI do it for you.

### Manual Scheduling
1. Go to the **Schedule List** section.
2. Add a new **Time Block**.
3. Select the **Date**, **Start Time**, and **End Time**.
4. Choose the **Subject** you will be studying and write a specific **Topic** (e.g., "Thermodynamics Chapter 3").
5. Save the block.

### Completing Tasks
As you finish studying a specific topic, click the checkbox next to the scheduled block. 
*Note: To maintain your Daily Streak, you must complete ALL scheduled blocks for that day!*

---

## ⏱️ 4. Using the Pomodoro Timer

The Pomodoro Timer is where the actual work happens. It uses focused work sessions followed by short breaks to keep your brain fresh.

1. Go to the **Pomodoro Timer** widget.
2. **Select a Subject:** Choose which subject you are about to study so the time counts towards its goal.
3. **Start the Timer:** The default focus time is usually 25 minutes. 
4. **Focus:** Work without distractions until the timer rings.
5. **Take a Break:** The timer will automatically switch to a short break (e.g., 5 minutes).
6. **Log Session:** When a focus session finishes, the app automatically logs the time. Your subject's "Completed Hours" will increase, and the data will be sent to your Analytics chart.

---

## 🤖 5. The AI Co-pilot (Your Second Brain)

Don't know what to study? Let the Gemini AI handle it.

1. Scroll down to the **AI Co-pilot** section.
2. The AI reads your current subjects, your goals, and looks at any study blocks you might have missed in the past.
3. Ask the AI for a plan (e.g., "Create a study plan for the next 3 days focusing on my weak subjects").
4. The AI will generate a smart, balanced schedule and can **automatically inject those time blocks** directly into your Schedule List.

---

## 🏆 6. Analytics & Gamification (XP & Streaks)

Neosis rewards consistency. We gamified the study experience to keep you motivated.

- **Daily Streaks:** If you complete 100% of the tasks you scheduled for a day, your streak goes up. If you skip a day you planned, the streak resets.
- **Analytics Chart:** Watch your daily study hours visually graphed over time. Identify which days you are most productive.
- **Rewards & Popups:** As you hit milestones, complete tough sessions, or maintain streaks, expect on-screen rewards and motivation to keep pushing forward.

---

## 💡 Pro Tips for Success
- **Be Realistic:** Don't schedule 10 hours of study in one day. Start with 3-4 realistic blocks.
- **Use the AI:** If you fall behind, don't stress. Use the AI Co-pilot to re-balance your schedule.
- **Always Use the Timer:** Make sure you link your Pomodoro timer to a specific subject so every minute you work is tracked!
